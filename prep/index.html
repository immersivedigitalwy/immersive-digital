06 · Local Conditions

Laramie & the high plains

At 7,200 feet, our weather does its own thing. A few extra minutes on these seasonal items keeps your exteriors and drone shots looking their best.

Weather & seasonal prep for Wyoming and Northern Colorado

Check whichever apply on your shoot date.

  • ❄️Snow & ice. Shovel and de-ice all sidewalks, driveways, porches, and decks. Brush snow off patio furniture, grills, and parked cars so the property reads clean.
  • 💨Wind. Laramie is one of the windiest spots in the country. Bring in or secure anything that blows around, patio cushions, flags, umbrellas, trampolines, and light decor, and skip staging with open umbrellas on gusty days.
  • 🌾Dormant or brown lawns. From late fall through spring, grass goes brown and dormant at this altitude. That's normal, we can enhance color in editing. Just clear leaves, cottonwood fluff, and dead growth so beds look tended.
  • 🥾Mud & spring melt. During thaw and mud season, hose off walkways, put down clean mats, and wipe muddy paw and boot prints near entries.
  • 🔥Wildfire smoke. Heavy late-summer smoke flattens light and hazes exteriors and aerials. If air quality is poor on your date, we may suggest rescheduling exteriors, twilight, or drone for a clearer day.
  • 🚁Drone days. If aerials are part of your package, high winds or storms can ground the drone for safety. A little date flexibility helps us catch the calm, clear window.

Before we arrive

The two that matter most:

Every light on, every blind and curtain open.
People and pets away for the full session.

Outside first

Park vehicles, tidy landscaping, sweep walkways, stage outdoor furniture, and put away all bins, tools, and toys. In winter, clear snow and ice.

Light it up

Turn on every light in the home. Open all blinds and curtains. Replace burned-out bulbs with matching sets.

Declutter & depersonalize

Clear countertops throughout. Remove personal items, family photos, and excess decor from every room.

Kitchen & bathrooms

Wipe all surfaces, store everyday items, close toilet lids, and display only fresh, neatly folded towels.

Pets & people out

Arrange for all occupants to be away for the full shoot. Pets away too, or securely locked in a separate room.
