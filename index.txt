OWNER PREP GUIDE · LARAMIE & NORTHERN COLORADO
Prepping your home for professional photos
A little preparation goes a long way. The more you check off before our photographer arrives, the smoother and faster your session runs, and the better your listing looks.
Please have all occupants, including pets, away from the property during the shoot, or at minimum secured in a separate room. If any rooms should be excluded, let us know as early as possible.

Quick Checklist
01 · EXTERIOR
Curb appeal matters
First impressions start outside. A tidy, welcoming exterior sets the tone for every photo that follows.
VEHICLES & STORAGE
	•	 ✓Park vehicles down the street or inside the garage
	•	 ✓Store trash and recycling bins in the garage or shed
	•	 ✓Put away garden hoses, tools, and outdoor equipment
	•	 ✓Store bikes, toys, and seasonal decor out of sight
LAWN & LANDSCAPING
	•	 ✓Mow the lawn and rake up leaves and cottonwood debris
	•	 ✓Pull weeds and remove any dead plants
	•	 ✓Refresh mulch or rock beds for a polished look
	•	 ✓Clear pine needles and foliage from the roof and gutters
CLEANING & STAGING
	•	 ✓Sweep walkways, porches, and all outdoor spaces
	•	 ✓Wash windows and glass doors
	•	 ✓Clean and straighten outdoor furniture
	•	 ✓Add pillows and open umbrellas to stage the space (calm days only)
02 · INTERIOR
Set the stage
A clean, bright, and decluttered interior photographs beautifully. These steps help every room look its absolute best on camera.
LIGHTING & WINDOWS
	•	 ✓Turn on every light, including closets, exterior, and bedside lamps
	•	 ✓Replace burned-out bulbs; use matching bulbs throughout
	•	 ✓Pull blinds down and open them all the same direction
	•	 ✓Pull curtains fully open to maximize natural light
	•	 ✓Clean mirrors and interior windows throughout
DECLUTTER & DEPERSONALIZE
	•	 ✓Clear all countertops and remove clutter
	•	 ✓Remove family photos, names, and personal or religious items
	•	 ✓Turn off and dust all TVs, monitors, and screens
	•	 ✓Store remotes, chargers, and cables out of sight
	•	 ✓Remove excess furniture, knick-knacks, and toys
	•	 ✓Empty all wastebaskets
QUICK ROOM RESETS
	•	 ✓Turn off all ceiling fans; store portable fans
	•	 ✓Vacuum carpets and polish hard floors
	•	 ✓Straighten furniture and open up crowded rooms
	•	 ✓Secure pets away from the property, or locked in one room
03 · KITCHEN
Clean & camera-ready
The kitchen is one of the most photographed rooms in any listing. Clear counters and sparkling appliances make an immediate impression on buyers.
CLEAR THE COUNTERS
	•	 ✓Remove food, dishes, and small appliances
	•	 ✓Store soaps, towels, and cleaning supplies; counters nearly bare
CLEAN EVERYTHING
	•	 ✓Wipe down all surfaces and appliances
	•	 ✓Sweep and mop the floor; remove worn rugs
	•	 ✓Put away draining racks completely
REFRIGERATOR & PANTRY
	•	 ✓Remove magnets, photos, and notes from the fridge door
	•	 ✓Tidy open shelves and the pantry in case they're captured
04 · LIVING AREAS & BEDROOMS
Inviting, not personal
These spaces should feel spacious and styled, but not overly personal. Thoughtful staging here helps buyers imagine themselves living in the home.
LIVING AREAS
	•	 ✓Minimize personal photos and decorative items
	•	 ✓Organize bookshelves and reduce visible clutter
	•	 ✓Remove excess furniture and accessories to open the space
BEDROOMS
	•	 ✓Make all beds and arrange decorative pillows neatly
	•	 ✓Store clothing, shoes, and laundry out of sight
	•	 ✓Tidy closets and declutter bedside tables
	•	 ✓Organize kids' rooms and clear items from under beds
05 · BATHROOMS
Spa-clean & simple
Bathrooms should look spotless and serene, think hotel-level clean. Remove the everyday clutter and keep only the essentials that look intentional.
TOILETS & TUBS
	•	 ✓Close all toilet lids before photography begins
	•	 ✓Leave shower curtains open; clear tubs and showers of clutter and soaps
TOWELS & LINENS
	•	 ✓Wash towels and rugs
	•	 ✓Hang towels neatly; fold or roll washcloths if displayed
CLEAR COUNTERS
	•	 ✓Store all countertop items
	•	 ✓Hide toilet brushes, plungers, and wastebaskets
06 · LOCAL CONDITIONS
Laramie & the high plains
At 7,200 feet, our weather does its own thing. A few extra minutes on these seasonal items keeps your exteriors and drone shots looking their best.
Weather & seasonal prep for Wyoming and Northern Colorado
Check whichever apply on your shoot date.
	•	❄️ Snow & ice. Shovel and de-ice all sidewalks, driveways, porches, and decks. Brush snow off patio furniture, grills, and parked cars so the property reads clean. 💨 Wind. Laramie is one of the windiest spots in the country. Bring in or secure anything that blows around, patio cushions, flags, umbrellas, trampolines, and light decor, and skip staging with open umbrellas on gusty days. 🌾 Dormant or brown lawns. From late fall through spring, grass goes brown and dormant at this altitude. That's normal, we can enhance color in editing. Just clear leaves, cottonwood fluff, and dead growth so beds look tended. 🥾 Mud & spring melt. During thaw and mud season, hose off walkways, put down clean mats, and wipe muddy paw and boot prints near entries. 🔥 Wildfire smoke. Heavy late-summer smoke flattens light and hazes exteriors and aerials. If air quality is poor on your date, we may suggest rescheduling exteriors, twilight, or drone for a clearer day. 🚁 Drone days. If aerials are part of your package, high winds or storms can ground the drone for safety. A little date flexibility helps us catch the calm, clear window.

BEFORE WE ARRIVE
The two that matter most:
Every light on, every blind and curtain open.
People and pets away for the full session.
Outside first
Park vehicles, tidy landscaping, sweep walkways, stage outdoor furniture, and put away all bins, tools, and toys. In winter, clear snow and ice.
Light it up
Turn on every light in the home. Open all blinds and curtains. Replace burned-out bulbs with matching sets.
Declutter & depersonalize
Clear countertops throughout. Remove personal items, family photos, and excess decor from every room.
Kitchen & bathrooms
Wipe all surfaces, store everyday items, close toilet lids, and display only fresh, neatly folded towels.
Pets & people out
Arrange for all occupants to be away for the full shoot. Pets away too, or securely locked in a separate room.
